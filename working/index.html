About Me

Hello! I’m Anurag Hruday Pirangi, a Machine Learning Engineer and Robotics Enthusiast with a passion for pushing the boundaries of technology. With a Master’s degree in Robotics from the University at Buffalo, I specialize in deep learning, computer vision, and autonomous systems. My academic foundation is further strengthened by a Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering from IIIT Guwahati, where I honed my problem-solving and engineering skills.

I’ve had the opportunity to contribute as a Machine Learning Engineer at Drishte A.I., where I developed machine learning models, optimized real-time analytics pipelines, and implemented edge AI solutions. Additionally, I advanced speech feature extraction techniques as a Research Intern at NIT Warangal, focusing on improving clarity and bandwidth in audio processing systems.

My projects include intelligent traffic signal control using reinforcement learning, autonomous navigation with LiDAR and SLAM, and a custom LLM for text-to-image generation. I am also pursuing research in background object detection, depth-aware object recognition, and augmented reality solutions for indoor environments.

Outside work, I enjoy exploring new technologies, engaging in outdoor adventures, and experimenting with creative tech projects. I believe in the power of curiosity and innovation to drive growth and make an impact.
